Our Story

Childers Speed Shop was originally founded 11 years ago by Thomas Childers, a single father who had two goals:

  1. support his two daughters to give them the best lives he possibly could
  2. live out his dream of building quality vehicles
Thus, Childers Speed Shop was born.

Today, we operate out of an 1,800 square foot shop offering expertise in automotive engine rebuilds, part machining, and building your project with absolute excellence in automotive performance.
