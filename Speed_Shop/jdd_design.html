Design Document


Site Title: Childers Speed Shop
Developer: David Dunham
Proposed URL: http://www.childersspeedshop.com



Purpose Audience Message Scope Goals Navigation Outline Bibliography
Purpose:
This site is brand new to the comapny. The main purpose is to bring attention to the company around the community it surrounds and grow the business as a whole. One of the things this company lacks is employees. The owner of this comapany wants to grow his business, gain employees and clientle alike.

"Serve the community with great service and low prices"
Target Audience:
This business is aiming to gain the attention of the community surrounding the company as well as car clubs, car enthusiasts, and amature racing teams.
Message:
The lack of community presence for this company is devestating to the amount of business the company could be doing. Creating a website for this company could quite possibly help build the company to a house hold name in the community it serves.
Scope:
This site has a newly formed logo, detailed backstory and contact information for customers to bring business to the company. It also covers the services offered and an information for the company to grow closer to its customers. The last page of the site is a list of links partaining to automotive interests.
Goals:
The most important issue for the owner of this company is to promote sales and services offered by the shop. Meanwhile, growing the company in revenue and personel, while creating more jobs in the community and better serving the customers as a whole.
Navigation:
Other than the navagation bar provided at the top of each page, I have given several navigation tools to the user. I have created links referencing different pages within the website, when it has been brounght up. Not only that, I have provided an email link on every page for the user to contact the owner of this company. I even prvided the same link hidden in the email graphic on the footer of the page.
Outline:
First things, first. I wanted the logo of the site to be the focal point of the users attention when they first visit. I wanted the content of the pages to be easy to read and have a color sceme that was easy on the eyes. I used darker colors with light text to do this.
Bibliography:
While building this site I had help from several resources including:
  • JavaScript for Web Warriors, 7th edition
  • w3schools
  • way2tutorial
  • Canva

My largest resource was definitely w3schools. This site was a huge help when I needed a reference on how to code something specific within the site. I went to this site more than any other.

Another good resource that I used on occasion was the way2tutorial site. I mainly used this site to figure out how to code internal links within a webpage.

The last source I used was Canva, a graphic design website. I used this site to create the webpage logo as well as the email graphic used within the footer. Canva has a lot of great resources to use that are free to use, create and distribute. The even have a paid subscription to utilize more resources as you wish.

The images used on the site are from the Childers Speed Shop facebook page as this is a real business. when I informed the owner of the company of this project he was more than grateful to let me use anything I needed to build this site.

Background colors and textures used are all from Visual Studio Code's style and color options. I randomly changed the site styles until I found the style I liked.
